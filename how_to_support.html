Ways You Can Support Mental Health

  • Listen without judgment when someone opens up to you.
  • Educate yourself on common mental health challenges and language.
  • Share trusted resources like hotlines or websites.
  • Encourage professional help and offer to help find it.
  • Promote mental wellness in your workplace, school, or community.
  • Model healthy coping strategies in your own life.
  • Support organizations and initiatives that raise awareness and fund services.

    Supporting Someone in Crisis

    • Stay calm and present. Let them know they are not alone.
    • If you believe someone is in danger, contact emergency services.
    • Know the hotlines and local services you can recommend or call.
    • Follow up with them after the crisis passes to show ongoing support.

    Promoting Mental Health in Your Community

    • Host or participate in mental health awareness events.
    • Use your social platforms to share accurate information and support resources.
    • Volunteer with local mental health organizations.
    • Advocate for mental health education and accessible care in schools and workplaces.
